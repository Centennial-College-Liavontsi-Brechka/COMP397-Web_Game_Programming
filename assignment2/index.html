SHEEP IN SHIPMars adventures

EPISODE I
GET ME BACK HOME

The humanity decided to continue Mars exploring missions.

By using a special spaceship, people wanted to test the safety of such flights, and, as a first flight, they took a sheep, named Sam, to be an experimental passenger.

However, during the flight, something went wrong and people lost a contact with the animal`s spacecraft.

Fortunately, Sam reached Mars, but his ship is out of fuel.

Help Sam to find a way to refill it or get back home.
